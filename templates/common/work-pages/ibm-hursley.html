IBM Innovation Centre - Hursley UK

In the summer after graduating from high school I worked as a software developer intern at IBM in the Innovation Center in Hursley, UK. While there I developed a scheduling viewer for the IBM Innovation Center office. The application was programmed in Javascript using the Dojo Toolkit, and is currently used to visualize IBM client schedules at the Innovation Center in Hursley.
