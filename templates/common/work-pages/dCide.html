dCide

In summer of 2012 I worked on an app in my free time called dCide. I worked with the others from my company inBenchon to build this simple utility app.

dCide rids you of the burden of choice by providing you with several tools to help you make decisions. Leave it up to luck or vote for your favorite choice, your call. dCide includes Yelp integration to help you make a choice if you need ideas.
